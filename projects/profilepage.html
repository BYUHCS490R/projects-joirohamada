Hello! Hola! こんにちは！ My name is Joiro Hamada & I want to get to know you better!

初めまして！Mucho gusto! I am Joiro Hamada. I was born in Springfield, Ohio, USA, 22 years ago. I was born on July 18, 2003. I am half Japanese/Mexican. My dad is from Osaka, Japan, and my mom is from Chiapas, Mexico.

When I was 2 years and a half years old, I moved to Osaka for a year, and then I moved to Chiapas in Mexico where I lived for 6 years. After living there for 6 years, because of my dad's job, we had to move to Baja California in northern Mexico where I lived for 3 years. After that I moved to Jalisco for another 6 years. After graduating from high school, I moved with my dad to Saitama, Japan, where I learned Japanese. Then I decided to go on a mission. I was assigned to serve in the Nagoya Japan Mission. 2 years later, I decided to move to Hawaii to get a degree in Computer Science here at BYUH where I am currently studying.

My hobbies are to listen and play music. I play the violin, guitar and piano, and I am currently the pianist for the Salsa Orchestra here at BYUH. I also like dancing to salsa, cumbia and bachata. I LOVEE to travel and to get to know new places. I am planning on going to Europe for the first time for spring break and I am very excited about it!
