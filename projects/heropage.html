Alex Turner

I would like to tell you a little bit about what kind of person my Alex Turner is.

Alex Turner is the main vocalist from Arctic Monkeys. He is originally from Sheffield, England and he loves The Strokes. He is a huge fan of Julian Casablancas. He started playing music because his father was also a music teacher.

He started his band when, one Christmas, his parents gave him his first guitar at the age of 17, a white Mexican Fender Stratocaster, and since then he and his high school friends have been writing songs together, creating one of my favorite albums. WPSIAMTWIN
