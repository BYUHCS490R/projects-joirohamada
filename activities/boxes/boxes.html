Box Model

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags? Block tags start on a new line and span the entire available width, while inline tags do not start on a new line and occupy the space they need.

What is the margin, border, and padding? Margin is the external space that separates one element from others. Border is the line that surrounds the content, and padding is the space between the content and the page edge.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS? px cm, in, %, em, rem, vh and vw

When would you use a figure tag? It is used to group images or other items with a description.
